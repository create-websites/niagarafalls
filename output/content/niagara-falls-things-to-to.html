Unforgettable Niagara Falls Experiences

Your guide to the most iconic tours, stunning viewpoints, and exciting attractions.

Iconic Tours & Experiences

Maid of the Mist / Hornblower

The quintessential Niagara experience. Don a poncho and journey into the heart of the falls. Feel the immense power and get soaked by the refreshing mist. An absolute must-do!

Location: USA & Canada

Journey Behind the Falls

Descend 125 feet and explore 130-year-old tunnels that lead to observation decks directly behind the thundering sheet of the Horseshoe Falls.

Location: Canada

Cave of the Winds

Get closer than you can imagine to the Bridal Veil Falls. Walk on a series of wooden decks, including the famous Hurricane Deck where you'll feel tropical-storm-like conditions.

Location: USA

White Water Walk

Take an elevator down to the river's edge and stroll along a boardwalk next to the raging Class 6 rapids of the Niagara River. A showcase of nature's raw power.

Location: Canada

Stunning Views & Evening Fun

Skylon Tower

Ride a glass-enclosed elevator 775 feet up for an incredible 360-degree bird's-eye view of the falls and the surrounding region.

Fireworks Display

On select nights throughout the year, the sky above the falls erupts in a spectacular display of fireworks. A magical end to any day.

Niagara Falls Illumination

Every evening, the three waterfalls are transformed into a multi-colored water and light masterpiece. The view is truly breathtaking.
