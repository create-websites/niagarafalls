Illumination Fireworks Night Tips

Experience the Magic After Dark

Discover the wonder of Niagara Falls as it transforms into a breathtaking spectacle of light and color each night.

The Nightly Falls Illumination

Every single night of the year, the three waterfalls are bathed in a rainbow of brilliant colors. Powerful LED lights located on the Canadian side project a stunning display onto the rushing water, creating a mesmerizing, year-round attraction that is completely free to enjoy.

The colors change throughout the evening and are often customized for holidays and special events, making each visit unique.

Spectacular Fireworks Over the Falls

On select nights, the sky above the falls comes alive with a world-class fireworks display. Launched from the Niagara Gorge, the explosions beautifully complement the illuminated falls below, creating a truly unforgettable multi-sensory experience.

This dazzling show is a highlight of the summer season and also runs during the Winter Festival of Lights and on other holidays. Be sure to check the schedule, as it is subject to weather conditions.

Tips for Your Night Visit

  • Check the Schedule: Illumination happens daily from dusk, but fireworks are seasonal. Always check the official Niagara Parks website for the latest schedules before you go.
  • Find the Best Spot: For the best ground-level views, find a spot along the railing in Queen Victoria Park (Canada). For an elevated view, consider the Skylon Tower or a Fallsview hotel room.
  • Dress Warmly: Even on summer evenings, the mist from the falls can make the air feel chilly. It's always a good idea to bring an extra layer or a light jacket.
