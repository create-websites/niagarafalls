Tips for Booking the Best View

  • Specify "Fallsview": When booking, ensure you select a "Fallsview" room. Not all rooms in these hotels face the falls. The Canadian side generally offers more direct and panoramic views.
  • Higher is Better: Request a room on the highest floor possible. This will give you a more expansive, unobstructed view over other buildings and trees.
  • Consider the Season: Views are spectacular year-round, but winter offers a unique icy landscape. Summer is busiest, so book well in advance for the best rooms.
