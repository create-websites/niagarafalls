Unforgettable Niagara Falls Experiences

Discover the top tours, breathtaking viewpoints, and thrilling attractions with our guide.

Iconic Tours & Experiences

Maid of the Mist / Hornblower

Experience the ultimate Niagara adventure by donning a poncho and venturing into the heart of the falls. Feel the incredible power and embrace the refreshing mist. A definite must for your itinerary!

Location: USA & Canada

Journey Behind the Falls

Descend 125 feet to explore tunnels that are over a century old, leading to observation decks located right behind the roaring Horseshoe Falls.

Location: Canada

Cave of the Winds

Experience the Bridal Veil Falls up close by walking on wooden decks, including the renowned Hurricane Deck that will immerse you in tropical storm conditions.

Location: USA

White Water Walk

Descend via elevator to the riverbank and meander along a boardwalk beside the tumultuous Class 6 rapids of the Niagara River, a display of nature's incredible strength.

Location: Canada

Stunning Views & Evening Fun

Skylon Tower

Take a ride in a glass elevator soaring 775 feet high for a breathtaking 360-degree panoramic view of the falls and the surrounding area.

Fireworks Display

Certain nights each year, the sky above the falls bursts into a dazzling fireworks show. A perfect way to end the day.

Niagara Falls Illumination

Each night, the three waterfalls become a dazzling display of colorful water and light, creating a truly awe-inspiring sight.
