Illumination Fireworks Night Tips

Experience the Magic After Dark

Experience the magic of Niagara Falls as it becomes a dazzling display of light and color every evening.

The Nightly Falls Illumination

Each night, all year long, the three waterfalls are illuminated in a vibrant array of colors. LED lights on the Canadian side cast a breathtaking spectacle onto the cascading water, forming a captivating, cost-free attraction that never fails to mesmerize.

Each visit is made unique by the changing colors throughout the evening, which are often tailored for holidays and special events.

Spectacular Fireworks Over the Falls

Certain evenings bring the sky above the falls to life with a spectacular fireworks show. Originating from the Niagara Gorge, the bursts of color perfectly enhance the glowing falls, resulting in a remarkable sensory experience that will not be easily forgotten.

Don't miss this spectacular show during the summer season, as well as the Winter Festival of Lights and other holidays. Be sure to check the schedule for weather-related updates.

Tips for Your Night Visit

  • Check the Schedule: The Niagara Parks website always has the most up-to-date schedules for daily illumination at dusk and seasonal fireworks. Be sure to check before visiting.
  • Find the Best Spot: For optimal views at ground level, locate a spot by the railing in Queen Victoria Park (Canada). For a higher vantage point, contemplate visiting the Skylon Tower or booking a room at a Fallsview hotel.
  • Dress Warmly: In the summer evenings, the mist from the falls can create a cool breeze, so it's wise to have an extra layer or a light jacket on hand.
