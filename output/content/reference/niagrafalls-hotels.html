Tips for Booking the Best View

  • Specify "Fallsview": When making a reservation, make sure to choose a 'Fallsview' room as not all rooms in these hotels have views of the falls. Typically, the Canadian side provides more direct and panoramic views.
  • Higher is Better: Ask for a room on the top floor for the best view without any obstructions from surrounding buildings or trees.
  • Consider the Season: The views are stunning throughout the year, but winter showcases a distinct frozen landscape. Make sure to book early for the most desirable rooms during the busy summer season.
